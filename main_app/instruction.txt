1.Build the image:                      docker build -t my-coda-app .

2.Push to a Registry (like AWS ECR):    docker push my-coda-app

3.Deploy to Kubernetes:                 kubectl apply -f deployment.yaml

4.Create the Load Balancer:             kubectl apply -f service.yaml

5.Get the URL:                          kubectl get service coda-app-service
(This will give you a long AWS URL you can paste into your browser!)
